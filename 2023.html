Chart 5: Bias Motive Coding
All 35 victims coded by bias motive evidence
Methodology: Confirmed = hate crime charges or explicit LE statement. Suspected = evidence of animus, no charge. No Evidence = documented alternative motive (IPV, robbery, mass shooting, police killing, random crime spree). Unknown = insufficient info or unsolved with no motive info.
