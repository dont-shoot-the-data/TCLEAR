Chart 5: Bias Motive Coding
All 34 victims coded by bias motive evidence
Methodology: Confirmed = hate crime charges or explicit LE statement (3 cases: Club Q shooting, Cherry Bush). Suspected = evidence of animus, no formal charge (3 cases: Ariyanna Mitchell — gender questioned before shooting; Fern Feather — trans panic defense; Shawmaynè Marie — shot after learning victim was transgender via dating site). No Evidence = documented alternative motive (9 cases: IPV, robbery, familicide, police-involved). Unknown = insufficient info or unsolved (19 cases).
